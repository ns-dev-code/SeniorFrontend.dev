# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Haan, kaafi baar hua hai. Mostly color contrast aur focus styles ke around.

Main isko kabhi ego battle nahi banata. Main designer ko real examples dikhaata hoon — contrast checker, keyboard demo, screen reader output.

Usually designers bhi accessibility ke against nahi hote, unko bas real impact clearly dikhana padta hai.
