# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Haan, aur honestly wo kaafi painful experience tha. Ek form flow production me tha jahan visually sab perfect lag raha tha, par screen reader user completely stuck ho jaata tha.

Issue ye tha ki error message announce hi nahi ho raha tha. QA testing me ye miss ho gaya kyunki sirf visual testing hui thi.

Us incident ke baad humne rule bana diya ki critical flows ke liye keyboard aur screen reader testing mandatory hai. Lesson ye tha ki accessibility bugs bhi functional bugs jaise hi treat hone chahiye.
