# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Focus management ko log underestimate kar dete hain, par ye accessibility ka heart hai. User ko hamesha pata hona chahiye ki wo ab kis jagah pe hai.

Modal open hota hai to focus uske andar jaana chahiye. Modal close hota hai to focus wapas trigger element pe aana chahiye.

Route change ke baad main focus ko main heading ya content pe move karta hoon. Agar focus random jagah chala jaaye, user lost feel karta hai.
