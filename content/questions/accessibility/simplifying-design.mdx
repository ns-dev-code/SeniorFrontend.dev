# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Haan, kaafi baar. Kabhi-kabhi design visually impressive hota hai par usability ke against hota hai.

Maine cases dekhe hain jahan complex hover interactions ya tiny click areas remove karne pade. Simple design often zyada accessible aur usable hota hai.

Accessibility ne mujhe simplicity ki value sikhayi hai.
