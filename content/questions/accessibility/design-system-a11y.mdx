# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Design system accessibility ke liye sabse powerful leverage point hota hai. Agar base components accessible hain, to product automatically kaafi accessible ho jaata hai.

Button, input, modal jaise primitives agar sahi bane hain, to feature developers ko har baar accessibility ke baare me dobara sochna nahi padta.

Main design system ko sirf visual consistency ke liye nahi, behavioral consistency ke liye design karta hoon.
