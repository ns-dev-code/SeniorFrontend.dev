# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Main accessibility ko lecture topic nahi banata. Main real demos dikhata hoon — keyboard-only usage, screen reader flow.

Jab developers khud experience karte hain ki cheez kaise break hoti hai, tab mindset naturally change hota hai.

Accessibility culture tab banti hai jab team empathy develop karti hai, rules se nahi.
