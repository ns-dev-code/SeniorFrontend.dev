# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Design systems repetition reduce kar dete hain. Agar ek accessible button already bana hua hai, to har team apna version nahi banati.

Isse accessibility bugs ek jagah fix hote hain aur sabko benefit milta hai. Centralization accessibility ka best friend hota hai.
