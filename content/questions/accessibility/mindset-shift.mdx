# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Sabse bada shift ye tha ki maine accessibility ko "special users" ke liye banana band kar diya. Main accessibility ko better UX for everyone ke roop me dekhne laga.

Jab tum aise sochte ho, to accessibility burden nahi lagti, balki product quality ka natural extension lagti hai.
