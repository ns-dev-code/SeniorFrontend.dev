# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Kabhi-kabhi compromise karna padta hai. Jaise heavy animation ko simplify karna ya initial load pe kam content dikhana. Main hamesha UX team ke saath align karta hoon aur explain karta hoon ki trade-off kya hai. Better thoda simple UX than laggy fancy UX. User ko smooth experience chahiye, not animation demo.
