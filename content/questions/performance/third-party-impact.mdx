# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Honestly, third-party scripts silent killers hote hain. Analytics, A/B testing, personalization — sab apni jagah important hain, par uncontrolled ho to site slow ho jaati hai.

Main hamesha ye check karta hoon ki script blocking to nahi hai. Async/defer use hota hai ya nahi. Aur main ye bhi dekhta hoon ki kya har page pe script zaroori hai. Kai baar scripts sirf specific flows ke liye required hoti hain. Performance ke naam pe analytics band nahi karte, par discipline zaroor rakhte hain.
