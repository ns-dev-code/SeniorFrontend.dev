# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Sabse frustrating hota hai jab issue intermittent ho. Kabhi hota hai, kabhi nahi. Aise cases me logs, metrics aur patience ka role aata hai. Main reproducibility create karne ki koshish karta hoon, even if thoda artificial ho. Performance debugging kabhi quick win nahi hoti. Ye ek investigative process hota hai.
