# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
LCP mushkil isliye hota hai kyunki wo usually “hero content” se tied hota hai. Aur hero content aksar business aur design driven hota hai — big images, banners, carousels.

Engineering solution simple hota hai, par decision complex hota hai. Agar hero image heavy hai, to ya to quality reduce karo, ya loading strategy change karo. Maine ye dekha hai ki LCP improve karna tab possible hota hai jab engineering aur design align ho. Sirf code change se har baar solution nahi nikalta.
