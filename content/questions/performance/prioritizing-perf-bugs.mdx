# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Main pehle impact dekhta hoon. Kya sab users affected hain ya sirf specific devices? Kya feature unusable ho gaya hai ya sirf thoda slow feel ho raha hai?

High impact performance bugs ko main functional bugs ki tarah treat karta hoon. Agar user click nahi kar pa raha ya page freeze ho raha hai, to wo bug hi hai. Low impact issues ko backlog me daalta hoon, par ignore nahi karta. Performance debt bhi debt hota hai.
