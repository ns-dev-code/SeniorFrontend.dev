# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
CLS ka sabse bada dushman hota hai “future changes”. Aaj layout stable hai, kal marketing banner aa gaya aur CLS explode ho gaya. Isliye main CLS ko sirf current UI ke liye fix nahi karta, future ke liye plan karta hoon.

Dynamic sections ke liye hamesha reserved space rakhta hoon. Maine ye bhi dekha hai ki CLS issues mostly “last minute content changes” se aate hain. Isliye performance review me layout stability bhi discussion ka part hota hai.
