# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Haan, ek baar rollback kiya hai. Ek new animation-heavy feature ship hua tha jo low-end devices pe UI freeze kar raha tha. Initially humne try kiya optimize karne ka, par issue quickly resolve nahi ho raha tha. 

Business impact zyada tha, to humne rollback ka decision liya. Rollback ka decision ego ka nahi hota. User experience zyada important hota hai than “feature shipped” pride.
