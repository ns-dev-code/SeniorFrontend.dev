# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Aajkal log bol dete hain ki Redux outdated hai, par real world me ye bilkul sahi nahi hai. Redux ka use-case aaj bhi kaafi strong hai.

Main Redux tab consider karta hoon jab: state bahut jagah use ho rahi ho, multiple teams same state pe depend kar rahe ho, aur debugging important ho. Redux ka biggest advantage mujhe lagta hai predictability. Actions aur reducers ke through flow samajhna easy hota hai, especially jab bug production me aaye.

Haan, chhoti apps ke liye Redux overkill ho sakta hai. Par enterprise scale pe, jahan cart, permissions, ya global workflows hote hain, Redux abhi bhi kaafi relevant hai.
