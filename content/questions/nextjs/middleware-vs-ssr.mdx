# Deep Answer

### Interview Answer (Aise bolna hai):
Middleware aur SSR logic ka use-case overlap lagta hai, par dono ka role alag hai.

Middleware main quick decisions ke liye rakhta hoon — auth redirect, geo-based routing, A/B test bucket decide karna. SSR logic heavy hota hai — data fetching, rendering, business rules. Main middleware me kabhi heavy computation nahi daalta, warna har request slow ho jaati hai.

Simple rule follow karta hoon: middleware = gatekeeper, SSR = renderer.
